About Me:

Although I enjoyed my work and genuinely enjoyed helping people, I found myself always looking for a challenge but not finding one. One day a friend recommended looking into Computer Science and software development. I went on to code academy and learned HTML5 and CSS3. I enjoyed it but had no idea where to go next. She then told me to look into Prime Digital Academy. It is definitely the best decision I’ve made.


My journey through Prime has allowed to me to utilize skills I’ve already had while also equipping me with the tools and skills I need to succeed as a software developer. While at Prime I’ve learned full stack development. I’ve learned new languages such as AngularJS, jQuery, JavaScript, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, and postgreSQL (and many more) and I have created web applications using these languages.
